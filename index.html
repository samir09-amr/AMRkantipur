About AMR Club

We are a dedicated group of health professionals and enthusiasts working to combat antimicrobial resistance through education and community engagement.

Our Mission to Combat AMR in Kantipur

The AMR Club of Kantipur was established with the goal of creating awareness about antimicrobial resistance and promoting responsible use of antibiotics in our community.

We conduct regular health camps, educational workshops, and awareness programs in schools and colleges to spread knowledge about AMR and its impact on public health.
